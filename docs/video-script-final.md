## POST-PRODUCTION NOTES

### Must-Include Elements

1. **"Gemma 4 E4B via Ollama"** — say verbally at least 2x (sections 3A, 5)
2. **Airplane mode toggle** — must be visible on camera (section 3D)
3. **Bounding boxes** — show spatial feedback working (section 3B)
4. **Voice feedback** — let app speak at least one full response
5. **Low-vision framing** — never say "blind" alone; always "blind and low-vision"

### Visual Guidelines

- **Opening 15 seconds:** Highest production value. No faces, just hands and craft.
- **Demo sections:** Clean, uncluttered screen recordings.
- **Stats cards:** Simple, readable, high contrast.
- **Close:** Emotional but not manipulative. Confident, not desperate.

### Audio Guidelines

- **Voiceover:** Calm, clear, professional. Not overly excited.
- **Music:** Subtle ambient/emotional. Never overpowers speech.
- **App sounds:** Include UI sounds to show responsiveness.

### Captions

- **REQUIRED:** Full captions for accessibility.
- Use high-contrast subtitle style (white text, dark outline).
